# openfhe 0.1.0

* Initial version.  Wraps the bundled OpenFHE library (v1.5.1) providing the
  BFV, BGV and CKKS homomorphic encryption schemes with an idiomatic R
  interface: encrypted vectors and matrices with native arithmetic and linear
  algebra operators, rotations and reductions, approximate evaluation of
  smooth functions, encrypted comparisons via scheme switching, CKKS
  bootstrapping, and serialisation of keys and ciphertexts.
